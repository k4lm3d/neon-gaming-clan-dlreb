Our Story

Founded in 2020, Neon Gaming Clan started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for competitive gaming. What began as casual weekend sessions quickly evolved into something much bigger.

Today, we're a thriving community of 50+ active members competing across multiple titles. From local tournaments to international leagues, we've built a reputation for tactical excellence and sportsmanship.
